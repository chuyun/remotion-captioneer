Everything You Need

    

      
🎬 14 Caption Styles
Word-highlight, karaoke, typewriter, bounce, wave, glow, pill, flicker, rainbow, scale, spotlight, and more.

      
🎤 5 STT Providers
Local whisper.cpp, OpenAI, Groq, Deepgram, AssemblyAI — pick your speed vs accuracy tradeoff.

      
🎵 Audio-Video Sync
Beat detection, volume hooks, energy analysis, timeline interpolation — all frame-accurate.

      
📦 7 Export Formats
SRT, VTT, ASS, TXT, word-level SRT & VTT, plus @remotion/captions compatibility.

      
🎨 24 Presets
Social, Podcast, Cinematic, Music, Tutorial, Minimal, Gaming, News, Education, Fun — ready to use.

      
📐 Layout Primitives
Container, Stack, Grid, Columns, Positioned, Overlay, GradientBg, FadeIn, SlideUp, Center.

      
😀 Emoji Reactions
Auto-generate emoji reactions from captions with 60+ keyword→emoji mappings.

      
🖥 CLI & Preview
10 CLI commands, live preview server, project scaffolder with npx captioneer init.
